Welcome to my blog. I've been meaning to start writing for a while, and this is me finally doing it.

I'll be writing about whatever I find interesting, ideas I'm working through, things I've been reading, projects I'm building. No particular niche, just things worth putting into words.

## Why write at all?

Writing forces me to be clear with myself. It helps me think through things and hash out the details. It forces me to look deeper into my understanding of things and I like that. 

## What to expect

Short posts, long posts, probably some technical stuff. Just a place to write my thoughts and feelings.
