Welcome to my blog. I've been meaning to start writing for a while, and this is me finally doing it.

I'll be writing about whatever I find interesting — ideas I'm working through, things I've been reading, projects I'm building. No particular niche, just things worth putting into words.

## Why write at all?

Writing forces clarity. You can hold a fuzzy idea in your head for weeks and think you understand it, then the moment you try to write it down, the gaps appear. I want to use this as a tool for thinking, not just broadcasting.

There's also something valuable about having a record. Not for anyone else necessarily — just for future me, who will have forgotten what he was thinking about in 2026.

## What to expect

Short posts, long posts, probably some technical stuff mixed in with less technical stuff. No schedule, no newsletter, no algorithm to feed.

If something here is useful or interesting to you, great. If not, that's fine too.
